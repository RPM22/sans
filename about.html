Welcome to sansundertale.cf!

Hello, my name is Rory McNamee, and I am a high school student interested in website design. I created a "company" called RTIMedia, which overlooks my various media endeavors. This beautiful atrocity is one of these endeavors. The website started as an inside joke between myself and some friends, featuring a GIF of Sans, the skeleton from Undertale, bouncing around like the DVD screensaver and extremely loud auto-playing music. You can view the original site here. After almost getting suspended from school for posting it into my Google Classroom class, I decided to put some more time into it and use it as a testing ground for my website development. The site very quickly became my most complex project by a lot, with new features like a canvas graphic EQ, Spotify and YouTube song selection, and loads of other additions. This site is in constant development, with a changelog posted here. The site is probably broken in some way at all times, so if you catch something broken, please fill out a feedback form here. Thanks for visiting!
